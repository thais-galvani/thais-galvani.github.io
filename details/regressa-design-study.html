Regressa

Why Regressa?

They create bags made from inner tuber, which have a leather-like appearance but are a sustainable alternative. Regressa is an innovative Brazilian brand that combines sustainability - by repurposing materials that would otherwise be discarded - with aesthetics, minimalist design and modern bags.

Improvements and visual decisions

For the visual redesign, I focused on black to reflect the inner tube material used in Regressa's products, paired with a soft green accent to subtly highlight sustainability without falling into clichés. I also designed custom icons to emphasize the materials used and incorporated them into the product cards and pages. Additionally, I created a “Big Numbers” module to showcase the real environmental impact Regressa has, making the brand’s sustainability efforts more tangible and engaging.

How could we measure the impact?

To measure the website’s impact on users’ perception of Regressa’s environmental focus, I’d track engagement with sustainability-related content, using heat maps to identify which sections draw the most attention. Metrics like time spent on these areas and interactions with eco-focused features would reveal how users perceive the brand’s environmental efforts. Additionally, surveys would help assess whether the site’s design influences their purchase intention.
