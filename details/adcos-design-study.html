ADCOS

ADCOS is a Brazilian dermocosmetics brand recognized for developing high-performance products for sun protection and skincare, with formulas tailored to the local climate and the diversity of Brazilian skin.

The big idea

To explore a more premium visual identity that conveys the lightness of its products while staying aligned with the brand’s existing photography style and social media communication.

I drew inspiration from ADCOS’ photography direction, using a soft and warm color palette that reflects the diverse skin tones the brand caters to. I also incorporated rounded shapes, referencing the brand’s logo and products such as compact powders, masks, and ADCOS’ signature stick sunscreen.

Product improvements

ADCOS strongly leverages influencer marketing, with TikTok filled with posts and reviews about its products. To capitalize on this, I integrated social commerce elements into the website. In the home hero section, the product image would be paired with a photo of an influencer applying it. On the product detail page, users would see various influencers with different skin tones showcasing the product in use. This helps potential buyers understand how the product adapts to different skin types, its texture, density, and finish.

Measuring Impact

The key metric would be analyzing how the integration of social media content impacts purchase decisions by tracking conversion rates for products featuring this type of content. Additionally, heatmaps and time spent on the page would help assess user engagement with these elements.
